The Wines

Montepulciano, Trebbiano & Cerasuolo

Three DOC wines that define Abruzzo's table — the Montepulciano reaches DOCG status in the Colline Teramane.

🍷

Montepulciano d'Abruzzo DOC

Full-bodied with dark cherry, leather and tobacco. Aged versions develop extraordinary complexity.

Deep ruby red
🍽️ Arrosticini, lamb, aged pecorino
🥂

Trebbiano d'Abruzzo DOC

In the right hands this becomes one of Italy's most profound white wines — mineral and age-worthy.

Straw yellow
🍽️ Brodetto, grilled fish, fresh pecorino
🌹

Cerasuolo d'Abruzzo DOC

Montepulciano made as a rosé — but with depth and structure most rosés don't approach.

Vivid cherry rosé
🍽️ Pasta chitarra, soft cheeses, antipasto
