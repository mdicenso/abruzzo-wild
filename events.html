Best season

Summer (June–September) is peak festival time. Winter has quieter but atmospheric events like the Farchie fire festival in January.

Entry & costs

Most sagre and traditional events are free. Music festivals and sport events may require advance booking. Budget €5–15 for food at sagre.

Getting there

A car is essential for village events. Many festivals are in small borghi with limited parking — arrive early or use shuttle buses where available.
