Best time to visit

May–June for wildflowers and mild temperatures. September–October for fewer crowds and harvest festivals. Avoid August peak season in coastal areas.

Getting around

A hire car is essential. Most villages and trails are not reachable by public transport. Book at Pescara Airport on arrival — roads are good but mountain roads require care.

Budget guide

Agriturismo: €50–80/night with dinner. Restaurants: €15–25/person. Trails: mostly free. Budget €80–120/day per person including accommodation and food.

Language

English is limited outside Pescara and L'Aquila. Learning 10 Italian food words will transform your experience. Locals are warm and patient with visitors who try.
