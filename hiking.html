Essential Tips
Footwear

Waterproof hiking boots required for any trail above 1,500m.

Weather

Start before 8am for long routes. Mountain weather changes fast.

Water

Carry at least 2L. Springs may be dry in summer.

GPS & Maps

Download offline maps on Komoot or Wikiloc. Signal patchy above 1,800m.

Emergency

Mountain rescue: 112 or Soccorso Alpino 118.
